3. Main dispatcher에 `lock` 명령 추가
4. `@exec_with_stdin` LLVM extern 선언 추가 (bootstrap/compiler.bmb)

## Verification & Defect Resolution

### 발견: exec_with_stdin declare 누락

`@exec_with_stdin` LLVM IR extern 선언이 bootstrap codegen에 없었음.
수정: `declare noalias ptr @exec_with_stdin(ptr, ptr, ptr) nounwind willreturn` 추가.
