# 261. Maximum Product Difference Between Two Pairs

The product difference between two pairs (a, b) and (c, d) is (a * b) - (c * d). Given an integer array nums, choose four distinct indices to maximize the product difference. Return the maximum value. (external, LeetCode #1913)

Input: first line is n, then n integers (one per line)
Output: maximum product difference

Example:
Input:
4
5
6
2
7
Output:
32

Example:
Input:
4
9
1
9
1
Output:
80
