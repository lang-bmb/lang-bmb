# 209. Maximum 69 Number

Given a positive integer num consisting only of digits 6 and 9. Return the maximum number you can get by changing at most one digit (6 becomes 9, or 9 becomes 6). (external, LeetCode #1323)

Input: a single integer num (1 <= num <= 10^4, only digits 6 and 9)
Output: maximum number after at most one change

Example:
Input: 9669
Output: 9969

Input: 9996
Output: 9999

Input: 9999
Output: 9999
