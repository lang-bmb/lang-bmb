# 184. Distribute Candies

Alice has n candies where n is always even. She wants to give her sister half. Find the maximum number of different types of candy her sister can receive. (external, LeetCode #575)

Input: first line n, then n integers (candy types, one per line)
Output: max number of different types

Example:
Input:
4
1
1
2
2
Output: 2

Explanation: Alice gives sister 2 candies. Sister can get types 1 and 2.

Input:
6
6
6
6
6
6
6
Output: 1
