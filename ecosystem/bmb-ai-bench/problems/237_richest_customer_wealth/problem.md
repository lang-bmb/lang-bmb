# 237. Richest Customer Wealth

You are given an m x n integer grid accounts where accounts[i][j] is the amount of money the ith customer has in the jth bank. Return the wealth that the richest customer has.

A customer's wealth is the amount of money they have in all their bank accounts. The richest customer is the customer that has the maximum wealth. (external, LeetCode #1672)

Input: first line m, second line n, then m*n integers (row by row, one per line)
Output: the maximum row sum

Example:
Input:
2
3
1
2
3
4
5
6
Output:
15
