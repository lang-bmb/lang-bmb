# 188. Maximum Product of Three Numbers

Given an integer array, find three numbers whose product is maximum and return the maximum product. (external, LeetCode #628)

Input: first line n, then n integers (one per line)
Output: maximum product of any three numbers

Example:
Input:
4
1
2
3
4
Output: 24

Input:
5
-1
-2
-3
1
2
Output: 6

Note: For negative numbers, the answer may be min1*min2*max or max1*max2*max3.
